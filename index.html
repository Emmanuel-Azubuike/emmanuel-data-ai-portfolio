Power BI Project

Pharma Sales & Inventory Dashboard

A demo dashboard focused on pharmaceutical sales, stock levels, product performance, and business insights.

Problem: A pharma business needs to understand sales performance and know which products are running low.

What I Did: I created a demo dataset, organized it in Excel, and planned a Power BI dashboard to track revenue, units sold, product categories, and inventory levels.

Tools Used: Excel and Power BI.

Insights: The dashboard will help identify top-selling products, low-stock items, monthly revenue trends, and products that may need restocking.

Status: Demo project in progress. Real dashboard screenshots and project files will be added after completion.

SQL Project

Pharma Inventory Analysis

A demo SQL project focused on stock tracking, reorder levels, and pharmacy inventory decisions.

Problem: A pharma business needs to know which medicines are selling, which are overstocked, and which are close to stockout.

What I Did: I designed a demo inventory dataset and will use PostgreSQL queries to analyze stock levels, sales movement, and reorder needs.

Tools Used: PostgreSQL and Excel.

Insights: The analysis will help flag low-stock products, slow-moving products, and categories that need better stock planning.

Status: Demo project in progress. SQL queries and result summaries will be added after completion.

AI Automation Project

Pharma Automation Workflow

A demo workflow showing how simple automation can reduce repeated manual tasks in a pharmacy or pharma business.

Problem: Pharmacy teams often repeat tasks like recording orders, sending follow-up messages, and updating simple tracking sheets.

What I Did: I designed a demo workflow where new order or customer information enters Google Sheets, then Zapier can trigger a Gmail response or notification.

Tools Used: Google Sheets, Zapier, Gmail, and AI prompting.

Insights: This kind of workflow can help save time, reduce manual errors, and improve customer follow-up.

Status: Demo project in progress. Workflow screenshots and a short explanation will be added after completion.
